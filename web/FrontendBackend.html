Frontend Versus Backend

Heated battle or something more?

Frontend Web Designers

Frontend web designers tend to be your artistic types, these people excel at design, they are typically more outgoing and in touch with what the client want to see. In the past frontend designers where just that designers not programmers but in the last few years more and more of the logic has moved to the front end due to large frameworks such as react and angular.

Backend Web Developers

Backend developers tend to be your math types, normally they are not at all interested in the way a site looks and are spending their time making sure that the app works, Backend developers are usually stronger in computer science and programming but fairly poor at design and making a site people want to use.
